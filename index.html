Our Goal

Voting is one of the most powerful tools that people can use to bring change in their communities, cities, states, and countries Only around ⅔ of eligible voters in the US end up casting a vote in 2020, so increasing voter participation and turnout is key to ensuring that legislative decisions continue to be influenced by the people. Without information about candidates, voters may feel unqualified to discuss election issues or not be in a position to make a decision on who to vote for. Reliable, easy to digest information is key to giving voters an idea of what each candidate is lobbying for and seeks to implement if they were to be elected. This can give more power to voters, who can be more confident in their voting choice

Our candidate bot gives both reliable and condensed information from each of their campaign websites to users about the candidates’ policies and how they have changed over the course of the campaigns, allowing users to more fully understand the changes at stake within their own communities. Based on what the users value, they can make more informed decisions with the information from our project.
